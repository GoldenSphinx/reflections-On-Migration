Our Story: Khaddija & Lola

From Reflections to Reality: Bringing the Project to Life

We have been driven for so long by both our shared and indvidual experiences as we've had the privilege to move about this world. Some of them were good, some were bad, some were absolutely frigtening; but we survived.

Many of our stories are shared (even till today) via musings and rantings on WhatsApp, but we know that we are not alone. Even for those who can't totally relate, there is still something to contribute.

This want to engage and collaborate with those far and near to us has fueled our passion for this project; and we hope it will encourage others to speak up and speak out.
